Taran
Quartet
Accordion · Guitar · Double Bass · Drums
Sound of Europe

Four musicians.
One sound.

Taran Quartet is led by accordionist and composer Anatol Taran. The programme combines his original works with carefully chosen pieces by Piazzolla, Galliano, and Chick Corea — all filtered through the quartet's own musical language.

"The accordion is not a folk instrument here. It leads, breathes, and improvises — on equal terms with the rhythm section." — Anatoli Taran

Warsaw, Poland · Available across Europe
