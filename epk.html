Biography
Anatoli Taran is a Belarusian accordionist based in Warsaw, widely recognized for his technically exceptional playing and innovative approach to the instrument. His distinctive sound bridges jazz, tango, and contemporary music — pushing the accordion far beyond its traditional boundaries.
"A fixture on the European concert circuit, performing in sold-out venues from France to Switzerland."
He is a member of juries at international accordion competitions and an endorsed artist of Victoria Accordions. Today based in Warsaw, he performs regularly at the city's most atmospheric venues while continuing to develop his ensemble projects for European audiences.
