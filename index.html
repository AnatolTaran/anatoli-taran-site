About

One of Eastern Europe's leading accordionists

Anatoli Taran is a Belarusian accordionist based in Warsaw, widely recognized for his technically exceptional playing and innovative approach to the instrument.

His distinctive sound bridges jazz, tango, and contemporary music — pushing the accordion far beyond its traditional boundaries.
